Our Services

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency medical services with quick response time.Emergency departments (EDs), also known as emergency rooms (ERs) or accident and emergency (A&E) departments, are equipped to handle a wide range of medical and psychiatric issues, from minor injuries like sprains and cuts to severe, life-threatening events such as heart attacks, strokes, major trauma, and severe allergic reactions.

Diagnostics

Advanced diagnostic facilities to ensure accurate treatment. When a patient arrives at an ED, a trained healthcare professional, often a nurse, performs a triage assessment. This process quickly evaluates the patient's condition and assigns them a priority level based on the severity of their illness or injury. Patients with the most critical conditions are seen first, which is why waiting times can vary.

Pharmacy

In-house pharmacy with a wide range of medicines.A key role of a pharmacist is to counsel patients on how to use their medications properly. This includes explaining the purpose of the medication, the correct dosage, potential side effects, and what to do if a dose is missed. They also provide advice on over-the-counter drugs and common health issues.
